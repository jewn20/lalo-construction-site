About LALO CONSTRUCTION

Shaping the landscape of construction through passion and excellence.

Built on Dreams, Solidified by Excellence

From humble origins and lean resources, a powerful vision took root. Fueled by relentless grit, an unyielding passion for electrifying, and the unwavering belief of our early supporters, LALO CONSTRUCTION dared to rise. Today, that same pioneering spirit ignites every project we undertake.

In a competitive arena, we’ve distinguished ourselves through consistency, dedication, and an obsession with quality. Our portfolio—delivered on time, built to last, and crafted with unparalleled care—reflects the trust our clients place in us.

Our Founder’s Journey

From humble beginnings, Dennis P. Lalo’s story is one of resilience and faith. Born into poverty, he took on responsibilities early, helping his family survive. Excelling academically, he earned a computer science degree and built a tech business, only to face setbacks. Guided by faith, he entered construction, founding LALO CONSTRUCTION to build not just structures but lasting impact.

Despite betrayals and losses, Dennis’s unwavering belief in service and God kept him grounded. Today, he leads with humility, supported by loyal friends and a commitment to excellence.
